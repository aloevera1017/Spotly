## Presentation Tips

**Timing**:
- Title: 30 sec
- Problem: 1 min
- Solution: 1 min
- Features: 2 min
- Demo: 3 min
- Tech/Impact: 2 min
- Future: 1 min
- Close: 30 sec
- Q&A: 3-5 min

**Delivery**:
- ✅ Make eye contact
- ✅ Speak clearly and confidently
- ✅ Show enthusiasm
- ✅ Use hand gestures
- ✅ Pause for emphasis
- ✅ Smile!

**Demo Tips**:
- Have backup screenshots
- Test everything beforehand
- Have data pre-loaded
- Keep browser windows ready
- Practice transitions

**Q&A Strategy**:
- Listen fully before answering
- Repeat question for audience
- Be honest if unsure
- Connect back to value prop
- Stay positive

Good luck! 🚀

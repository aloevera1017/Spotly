## 📄 How to Export to PDF

### Method 1: Print Button (Easiest)
1. Click the **"🖨️ Print PDF"** button at bottom
2. In print dialog, select **"Save as PDF"**
3. Choose destination and click **Save**
4. Done! ✅

### Method 2: Browser Menu
1. **Mac:** Press `Cmd + P`
2. **Windows:** Press `Ctrl + P`
3. Select **"Save as PDF"** as destination
4. **Important Settings:**
   - Layout: **Landscape**
   - Paper size: **Letter** or **A4**
   - Margins: **None** or **Minimum**
   - Background graphics: **✅ Checked**
5. Click **Save**

### Method 3: Chrome Specific
1. Open in **Google Chrome**
2. Press `Cmd + P` (Mac) or `Ctrl + P` (Windows)
3. Destination: **Save as PDF**
4. More settings:
   - Layout: **Landscape**
   - Background graphics: **✅ On**
   - Margins: **None**
5. Click **Save**
